Considerations

The following proposals are here to initiate debates, they are obviously not meant to be used as is (unless you really appreciate my talent in this matter :-$). Here are some things that were considered to create these images:

  • blue might not be a good choice for a logo as it is a color that the eye tends to ignore,
  • the examples use green and red as they are colors associated with unit tests (and with JUnit),
  • green is the major color as it is the color we expect to see more often when using MoreUnit,
  • the plus sign is an attempt to have something else that just text, and it is the only thing I could find linked to MoreUnit.
